Where should the rail sit, and does the panel overlay content?

All options use the same glass-rounded-panel style. The difference is placement and how the main content reacts.

A  ·  Rail flush-left, panel overlays

Rail is always in the left gutter (content reserves space). Panel floats overtop — no layout shift when it opens.

B  ·  Rail flush-left, panel pushes content

Rail in gutter. Opening the panel shifts main content to the right — same behavior as today, re-styled.

C  ·  Rail floats inset, panel overlays

Rail and panel both fully overlay content — content starts at 0, rail floats on top with a margin. Maximum content width.
