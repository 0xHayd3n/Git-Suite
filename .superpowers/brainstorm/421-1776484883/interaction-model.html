How should the sidebar panel open?

The rail will always be a floating rounded panel. The question is how the expanded panel appears.

A  ·  Hover reveals panel
panel appears on hover

Rail floats. Hovering the rail reveals the panel as an overlay. Click to pin/unpin.

B  ·  Click toggles panel
click icon to show/hide

Rail floats. Clicking an icon toggles the floating panel beside it. Same behavior as now, just re-styled.

C  ·  Always visible, both float
always open by default

Both rail and panel always float. Panel is always open; toggle to collapse entirely.
